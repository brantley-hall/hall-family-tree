Family History Word Document Parser

How to Use This Tool

  1. Open your family history Word document
  2. Select all text (Ctrl+A) and copy (Ctrl+C)
  3. Paste the text into the text area below
  4. Click "Parse Document" to extract family information
  5. Review the extracted data and export to your Hall Family Tree

Tip: The parser looks for names, dates, locations, and family relationships in your text.
